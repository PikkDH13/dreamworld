Welcome to the Dream

This is a Towny & Economy survival server. But the rules of physics are merely suggestions.

The Canvas

  • Build in the Wild, protected by Towny.
  • Gather materials in the Resource world.
  • Ascend ranks to unlock the Nether.
  • Survive to reach The End.

The Veins

  • The economy is entirely player-driven.
  • Earn money through Jobs.
  • Buy and sell via Chest Shops.
  • Find Capsules randomly while working.

The Anomalies

  • Capsules contain rare, glitched tools.
  • The Tether enchantment defies durability.
  • Items fracture and freeze when broken (Ghost Mode).
  • Repair them with Tether Coils to keep them alive.

The Foundry

Gather Copper and String. They are the blood of this world.
Refine them. Bind them. Stabilize the dream.
