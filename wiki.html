The Laws

Physics works differently here, but conduct remains absolute.

  • No Hacking or Exploits: Do not manipulate the code of the dream.
  • Respect the Drift: Hate speech and harassment cause instability. Be decent.
  • Griefing: Allowed in the Wild, forbidden in Towns. Protect your land.
  • Lag Machines: Any attempt to crash the reality will result in a ban.

Towny & Anchors

To survive without fading, you must anchor yourself. A Town is a protected area where you and others can build safely.

Getting Started

Type /t new [Name] to create a town (Costs Money).
Type /t invite [Player] to grow your population.

Upkeep: Towns require gold to stay materialized. Ensure your Town Bank (/t deposit) is funded, or your town will fall.

Jobs & Economy

The economy is entirely player-run. There are no Admin shops. The value of an item is what another dreamer is willing to pay.

Employment

Join up to 3 jobs (depending on rank) using /jobs browse.
Earn money by performing actions: Mining, Woodcutting, Hunting, etc.

Capsules

While working, you may find Capsules. These are rare drops containing items from outside the normal loot table.

The Anomalies

Capsules often contain tools with the Tether enchantment. These items defy standard durability rules.

⚠️ Fracture Warning

When a Tethered item reaches critical durability (< 10), it will Fracture.

A Fractured item loses all stats and enchantments. It becomes useless "Ghost Gear" until repaired. It does not break, but it cannot be used.

To repair it, you must use a Tether Coil.
